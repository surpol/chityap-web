Privacy Policy

Introduction

ChitYap is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you share images and voice notes.

Information We Collect

We collect information from your Apple ID (name, email) when you sign in, your shared images and voice notes, device information, and usage data to provide our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to authenticate you via Apple Sign In, enable sharing images and voice notes with friends, provide and improve our services, and send updates.

Your Media

Images and voice notes you share are stored securely. Your content is only visible to friends you choose to share with.

Sharing & Security

We do not sell your data. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your images, voice notes, and personal information.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your personal information and media at any time through your account settings or by contacting us.

Contact

Questions? Email us at privacy@chityap.app
